Data Exploration and Visualisation

This visualisation project focuses on the IMDB 5000 Movies dataset from Kaggle. Its objective is to provide an easy to interact one stop solution for its users. Target audience are mainly the Movie Creators (like producers directors etc.) and Movie Viewers (the movie watchers) both of the users have different perspective to look at this data. Like Creators will be more focused on the relation between the budget they invest and the gross their movie can earn or which genre is more profitable. And Viewers will like to watch best movies based on their mood or taste of genre. This project is an effort to bring interactiveness and a good user experience to its users. It is developed in D3.js, HTML, CSS, jquery.
